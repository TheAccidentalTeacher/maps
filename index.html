WHY PLAY THIS?

Learn to read coordinates while racing against time! Build your geography skills and compete for the highest streak!

📚 QUICK COORDINATE GUIDE:
Example: 40.71° N, 74.01° W
• 40.71° N = 40 degrees North of equator (New York area)
• 74.01° W = 74 degrees West of Prime Meridian
• This is New York City! 🗽
🎮 HOW TO PLAY:
1️⃣ You'll see coordinates like 40.71° N, 74.01° W
2️⃣ Think about which region they point to
3️⃣ Click on the map where you think it is
4️⃣ You have 60 seconds - hints appear at 30s and 15s
5️⃣ Get it right to build your streak for bonus XP!
💡 BEGINNER TIPS:
• Look at the first number (latitude) - is it close to 0° (equator) or 90° (poles)?
• Check N/S/E/W to know which hemisphere
• Don't stress about being exact - within 500km counts!
• Use hints if you're stuck - learning is the goal!
